About Us

It has been well documented by the press and consumer watchdogs that the automotive repair industry is fraught with problems. Lack of transparency, poor quality labour, and wasted time taking cars to garages have all led to a large amount of mistrust in the industry.

With a background in Automotive and Technology the team at ClickMechanic decided that there was a better way and in 2012 ClickMechanic was born.
