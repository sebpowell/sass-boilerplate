Information collected

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Information that you provide by filling in forms (or otherwise posting) on clickmechanic.com (the Website) or information that you ask us to collect from another site.
  • Vehicle information obtained from third parties based on a Vehicle Registration Mark provided by you, including but limited to: vehicle details; vehicle history; vehicle tax status and renewal dates; and vehicle MOT status, history and expiry date.
  • If you contact us, we may keep a record of that correspondence.
  • Your correspondence, via our site or associated phone numbers, with other users of our site or Mechanics in the Network.
  • We may also ask you to complete surveys that we use for research purposes, although you do not have to respond to them.
  • Details of your visits to our site including (but not limited to) traffic data, location data and the resources that you access on our site (as well as times and dates related to these).

When using the Website or Service you will be requested for necessary personal information. This information will only be used to help administer any services you have requested, and to inform you of other services which we feel will be of interest to you.

Unless we receive a statement from you explicitly stating you do not want to receive electronic communications it will be deemed that the receiver of the electronic communication has consented to receiving such a communication. You have the ability to opt-out of receiving electronic communications at any point of using our service. This opt-out can be found on the footer of any marketing communication sent to you.

We have taken measures to ensure that information supplied to us is held securely. Therefore we may occasionally request proof of identity before allowing access to any of this personal information.

You have the right to access any personal information supplied to ClickMechanic and change any inaccuracies held. Registration to the Website

The website will collect email addresses in order to send registrants their chosen password, which will enable member’s online access to the website. The user can access this information at any time in order to update any of their details. Other registration information is collected in order to generate a profile of the visitors using the ClickMechanic website. In the course of providing quotations on behalf of the Network the Website will collect names, email addresses, vehicle details and details of the mechanical work desired. We may use this information to send you useful emails relating to your quotation, vehicle or desired mechanical work.

Requests to the Network

In the process of requesting a Mechanic through the Service, we will gather personal information including names, addresses, contact details, and vehicle details. In addition to the uses outlined elsewhere in this Policy, we may share any of these details with any Mechanics in the Network for the purposes including: deciding whether to accept a request, fulfilment of the subsequent Booking, ordering of parts, offering opinions related to the request, or any other reasonable purpose.

ClickMechanic does not store the credit/debit card information necessary to collect payments, however we use a PCI-DSS compliant third party to store these details on our behalf. Cookies

When you visit ClickMechanic.com we issue a "cookie" file which is stored on your browser or the hard drive of your computer. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer’s hard drive. Cookies are widely used in order to make websites work, as well as to provide information. Cookies enable us to: improve the use of our Website; to recognise your computer; to store settings to help facilitate your use of our Website; and to deliver a better and more personalised service.

Some of the cookies used are essential for the Website to operate. You may block all or some cookies from this Website. However, if you use your browser settings to block cookies (including essential cookies) you may not be able to access ClickMechanic.com. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies as soon as you enter our Website.

For more information about cookies or for details on deleting them or refuse their installation on your computer, visit http://www.aboutcookies.org
